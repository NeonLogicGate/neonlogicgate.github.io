Privacy Policy

Neon Logic Gate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile applications and games.

1. Information We Collect

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Optional Permissions: Some apps may request permissions (like storage or camera) only for core functionality. These are used solely within the app and not transmitted to us.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance. This data cannot identify individual users.

2. Third-Party Services

Our free apps may include third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying ads in some apps. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand app usage patterns.

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data processed by our apps. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

Our apps are suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
